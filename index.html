La Comtesse d'Escarbagna

This play by Molière, La Comtesse d'Escarbagnas, is one of his least known works. It wasn't published during Molière life time and after reading it you will understand why. It certainly doesn't measure up to the other works that are still being presnted today.

This play was chosen for this project as a means of demonstrating that the creating of interlinear text and syncing it with audio can be done using readily available open source tools.

The play met serval criteria:

  • All the elements had to either be in the public domain or relased under the proper use licenses.
  • An audio recording of decent quality.
  • The text needed to be in a useable format.
  • The text and audio had to agree in order for the syncing to work.
  • And it should be a good length, between 25 to 35 minutes.
  • A bonus was that it is by Molière which has great name recogination.

Translation

The play is machine translated using the Google Chrome browser. Yup, not nearly as good as an intelligent human translation would have been, but it is fast. This is a major trade off and the where I get the most negatives reactions. So for all groaners I have the solution; which is to crowd souce a better translation. Here is a list of the scenes which link to a Google Spread sheet. When you see a tranlation that can be improve, go to the spreadsheet; find the word or phase and enter it into column C. You can leave a comment expalining your the reason. When you make an edit and/or comment the page editor will be notified.
