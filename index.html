La Comtesse d'Escarbagna

This play by Molière, La Comtesse d'Escarbagnas, is one of his least known works. It wasn't published during Molière life time, and after reading it you will understand why. It certainly doesn't measure up to the other works that are still being presnted today. This play was chosen for this project as a means of demonstrating that the creating of interlinear text and syncing it with audio can be done using readily available open source tools. The play met serval criteria:
  • All the elements had to either be in the public domain or relased under the proper use licenses
  • An audio recording of decent quality needed to be available
  • The text needed to be in a useable format
  • The text and audio had to agree in order for the alignment and syncing to work
  • And it should be a good length, between 25 to 35 minutes
  • A bonus was that it is by Molière which has great name recogination.
